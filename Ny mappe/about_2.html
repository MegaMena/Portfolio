About Me


My name is Milena Hundt, preferable called Mena.

I'm a 19-year-old multimedia design student and I'm currently preparing for my 1. semester exam!

I love to create, illustrate and design. To me, work doesn't feel right if I can't let out my creativity and immerse in a stream of ideas and fantasy.

You can find my school portofolio and my personal portofolio in navigation bar above. Feel free to take a look!

Why I study multimedia design

The reason why I study multimedia design in the first place, is because I love to create and I love to realize my ideas.

I have drawn ever since I can remember, so not doing it would just feel off. To me, creating something is like opening a little part of my inner universe. There are so many ideas that want to come to life, and this is the place where they can!
